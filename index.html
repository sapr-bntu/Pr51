Welcome to Pr51 Official page.

The Pr51 is the the most popular image viewer with GNU GPL License.

Soon our product get ready to start on Android!

Why Pr51?

Because this viewer is a very mysterious as an Area 51, very secret and mysterious american military base...

Main functionality

With Pr51 You can:
1. Open and view folders with images.
2. Set tags and rate your favourite pictures or photo.
3. Friendly interface and great usability satisfy any person, even most versed.
